The Joint Venture

Two complementary strengths, finding each other

Spider & Silk Supply is the research and production entity — headquartered in Taiwan, with a medtech subsidiary in Australia and operations across Europe. It breeds the spiders, harvests the fibre, and develops the science that turns it into something useful.

Dubai Silk Holdings is the joint-venture vehicle — a consortium of investors, designers, and medical researchers centred in Dubai, with partners across the UAE. It brings the capital, the market, and the craft.

Together they form a cross-jurisdictional innovation platform: a rare fibre, the science to engineer it, and two industries waiting for exactly what it offers. Neither could build this alone.

Our Commitments

Genuine differentiators, not marketing

Sustainability by nature

Spider silk is naturally produced, biodegradable, and renewable. The environmental case isn't bolted on — it's intrinsic to the material itself.

Ethical production

Our farm is built around the careful, individual housing of the spiders and traceable handling of every fibre from harvest to finished product.

Scientific rigour

Medical applications follow a defined research-ethics framework, with transparent methodology and a commitment to peer-reviewed publication.
